Worthing Cyclonauts

Social, Riding

All are welcome on our fun, social rides. We ride at the pace of the slowest, and pause regularly at the top of big hills to give everyone a chance to get their breath back (faster folks, bring a jacket so you don't chill off!).

We always have at least one cafe stop thanks to our club sponser PastryPicker.

Please Note that our rides cover certain minimum distances so be sure you are easily capable of those distances. You are still responsible for your own safety and wellbeing. Helmets encouraged!

Check out our upcoming rides below for more information.
